Most Wanted

What design formats are you OK with?

We accept pretty much everything.

Specifically, for layered designs:

  • Sketch and PSD,
  • AI, Adobe XD and INDD,
  • PNG, EPS, TIFF, etc.

For online design software:

  • Zeplin, Figma, Avocode, InVision and all similar formats.

For non-layered design files:

  • JPEG, PDF or any other with restrictions characteristic of non-layered formats.

Even existing markup is OK.
